Founder’s Story

BridgeAccess was founded by Mr. Nasser Mattar Al Tooqi, a visionary with a deep understanding of logistics and international trade. With over 15 years of industry experience, Mr. Nasser has built a business rooted in trust, reliability, and innovation. His mission is to empower Omani logistics to reach global standards while staying committed to service excellence and community impact.

Import & Export

We facilitate end-to-end import and export logistics, including trade documentation, customs clearance, port handling, and last-mile delivery. Whether you are trading regionally or internationally, we ensure your cargo moves smoothly, securely, and efficiently.

Containers

We provide full-service container logistics: FCL/LCL cargo handling, tracking, storage, and inland transport. Our advanced container management ensures real-time visibility and optimal movement at all times.

Local & International Delivery

BridgeAccess ensures fast and secure delivery services across Oman and to international destinations. From courier-sized shipments to bulk freight, our network guarantees reliability and time-sensitive execution.

Coordination

Our coordination service synchronizes suppliers, logistics providers, customs, and warehouses to ensure seamless movement and real-time responsiveness. We prevent delays before they start and keep your supply chain flowing.

Procurement & Sourcing

Our team sources quality suppliers and products tailored to your business needs. With regional and global reach, we negotiate the best terms and ensure reliable supply through vetted vendor networks.

Customs Clearance

We manage customs formalities, approvals, and duties with precision. Our team ensures you meet all Omani and international regulations for a smooth customs experience — saving you time and avoiding penalties.
